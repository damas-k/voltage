📐 Conduit Bending Calculator
Calculate take-up, shrink, offsets, saddle bends, and rolling offsets for EMT and rigid conduit.
Inputs
How It Works
Formula: Mark Location = Stub Height − Take-Up
Take-up is the amount of conduit the bend "uses up."
Measure from the end of the conduit to your mark, then align the mark with the bender's arrow/notch.
Inputs
How It Works
Distance = Rise × Multiplier
Shrink = Rise × Shrink/inch (total conduit run gets shorter)
Make M1, measure distance, make M2. Both bends same angle, opposite direction.
Inputs
How It Works
Center mark (C) is placed at the center of the obstruction.
Two outer marks are placed symmetrically on either side.
Center bend is 2× the angle; outer bends match the chosen angle.
Inputs
How It Works
Diagonal = √(Rise² + Run²)
True Offset = Diagonal (used for bending like a regular offset)
Angle = arctan(Rise ÷ Run)
Then use the offset multiplier for that angle to find distance between bends.

⚡ Free Tier Limits on OpenRouter
• Rate limited on free models during peak hours
• If you get an error, just wait 2 minutes and retry

⚠️ Limitations to Know
• PDF extraction works best on text-based PDFs, not scanned images
• Max 6,000 characters of your upload is sent to AI per generation
• AI chat is capped at 10 messages history to save tokens
• All data lives in browser localStorage — clearing cache erases everything
• Export your data regularly via Settings → Export Data to back it up
💡 Tips to Save API Usage
• Use built-in Study Topics question banks whenever possible
• Only upload files when you have specific material to quiz from
• Keep AI chat questions short and specific
• Enable rate limits in Settings to avoid burning through quota fast
